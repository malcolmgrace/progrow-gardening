Garden Maintenance in Barrhaven, Nepean & Ottawa South

A healthy garden takes consistent care throughout the season. We serve homeowners across Nepean, Barrhaven, Kanata, Manotick, and Riverside South, handling the groundwork on every visit: weeding, pruning, fertilizing, and pest management. Every maintenance visit is overseen by our Horticulturist, Malcolm Grace. Learn more about our team.

Weeding, Raking, and Cultivating

Clean beds and well-worked soil are the foundation of a healthy garden. Our team removes weeds, rakes away debris, and cultivates the soil to improve aeration and water absorption. It's the kind of regular groundwork that keeps your garden looking tidy and your plants growing well. For severe or recurring weed problems, see our dedicated weed control service.

Fertilizing and Disease/IPM Solutions

We apply fertilizers matched to your plants' needs and use Integrated Pest Management (IPM) to stay ahead of diseases and infestations. In Ottawa's climate, timing matters. Spring soil prep after the last frost sets the tone for the whole growing season. We keep interventions targeted so your garden stays healthy without unnecessary treatments.

Our Story

Ottawa's Trusted Gardening Professionals

ProGrow Gardening is a locally owned, owner-operated garden maintenance company based out of Barrhaven Ontario, serving Ottawa South and the surrounding communities. We work on these principles: arrive to a job fully prepared, be fully transparent with clients on any unexpected delay or extra work involved (gardens are full of surprises), and take care of every property the way we'd want our own looked after.

ProGrow was founded by Malcolm Grace, a Horticulturist and graduate of the Horticultural Industries program at Algonquin College. Before starting ProGrow, Malcolm built his experience across a wide range of landscape gardening work, including tree pruning, shrub shaping, garden design, planting, and full seasonal maintenance programs. He has led garden maintenance teams, worked with students learning the trade, and trained staff at all levels. That background in both the hands-on work and the people side of it is what drives the standard of care ProGrow brings to every property.

That background shows up in practical ways: plants selected for how they actually perform in Ottawa's Zone 5b climate and clay-loam soil, pruning timed to each plant's biological cycle, weed control using Fiesta iron-based herbicide approved under Ontario's cosmetic pesticide regulations, and pest management through Integrated Pest Management (IPM) principles that address root causes rather than symptoms.

ProGrow has been serving Ottawa homeowners since 2017. Every property gets the same standard of work, and every visit is done by our own team -- not subcontractors. Clear communication, consistent results, and no shortcuts.

How We Work

The Approach Behind Every Visit

Soil-First Philosophy

Most garden problems start underground. We assess soil conditions before making recommendations, and we amend Ottawa's clay-loam soil with organic matter rather than fighting its natural composition. Healthy soil produces plants that need less intervention over time.

Integrated Pest Management

We apply IPM principles across all maintenance work: identifying the root cause of pest and disease problems, monitoring on every visit, and treating only when thresholds are exceeded. Cultural controls come first. Chemical intervention is a last resort, not a default.

Eco-Friendly Weed Control

ProGrow uses Fiesta iron-based herbicide as our primary broadleaf weed treatment. It is registered with Health Canada, approved under Ontario's cosmetic pesticide regulations, and safe for families and pets once dry. For garden beds, we rely on manual removal and strategic mulching rather than chemical treatment.

Timing Matters

Ottawa's growing season runs from May through October. Pruning, planting, fertilizing, and weed treatment all have windows where they are most effective. We schedule work around those windows, not around what is convenient to book. The result is better outcomes for the same effort.

Right Plant, Right Place

The most common source of garden problems is a plant in the wrong location. We select species matched to your actual soil, light, and moisture conditions -- not what looks good at the garden centre. Plants in the right place establish faster, need less maintenance, and last longer.

No Subcontractors

Every ProGrow visit is carried out by our own team. We do not send subcontractors to properties. That means consistent standards, consistent communication, and a team that knows your garden from the previous visit and can track how it is progressing over the season.

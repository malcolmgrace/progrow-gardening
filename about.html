Our Company

Reliable Lawn & Garden Services

Ottawa's Trusted Gardening Professionals

ProGrow Gardening is a locally owned and operated lawn and garden maintenance company proudly serving the Ottawa region. We specialize in transforming and maintaining outdoor spaces with meticulous attention to detail, sustainable practices, and a commitment to enhancing the natural beauty of every property we work on.

Founded by Malcolm Grace, a graduate of the Horticultural Industries program at Algonquin College in Ottawa, ProGrow Gardening is built on a foundation of expert knowledge and hands-on experience. Malcolm’s formal education in horticulture, combined with his extensive background in lawn and garden care, enables us to offer clients a high level of professionalism, craftsmanship, and botanical expertise.

With years of experience in maintaining and improving residential and commercial outdoor spaces, we take pride in delivering high-quality workmanship and exceptional customer service. Every project is approached with care, precision, and a personalized touch to ensure the best possible results for our clients.

Our Story

Ottawa's Trusted Gardening Professionals

ProGrow Gardening is a locally owned, owner-operated lawn and garden company serving Ottawa South and the surrounding area. We work on a straightforward principle: do the job properly, be honest with clients, and take care of every property the way we'd want our own looked after.

ProGrow was founded by Malcolm Grace, a certified horticulturist and graduate of the Horticultural Industries program at Algonquin College. That background in plant science, soil health, and landscape management means you get real answers, not guesswork.

With years of hands-on experience across residential properties in Ottawa, Malcolm built ProGrow around getting the details right. Clear communication, consistent work, and no shortcuts. That's what keeps our clients coming back and referring us to their neighbours.

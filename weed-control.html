Common Questions

Weed Control FAQ

Is Fiesta safe for pets?

Yes. Fiesta is an iron-based herbicide and is considered safe for pets and children once the treated area has dried, typically within a few hours. It does not contain the synthetic chemicals found in conventional herbicides like 2,4-D.

How quickly does Fiesta work?

Most broadleaf weeds show visible browning and die-back within 3 to 7 days of application, depending on temperature and weed species. Dandelions typically respond within a week under good conditions.

How many Fiesta applications do I need?

For most Ottawa lawns, two to three applications per season gives good coverage. Heavy infestations may need an initial treatment followed by a follow-up visit two to three weeks later.

When is the best time to treat weeds in Ottawa?

Dandelion season in Ottawa peaks in May. Treating early in the season, before weeds go to seed, gives the best results and reduces how many return the following year.

Ottawa - Referral Partnership for Landscape Contractors

We Send You Clients.
You Send Us Clients.

ProGrow Gardening is building a network of trusted landscape service providers across Ottawa. Clear referral agreement, low fee, and a defined window after which the client is fully yours. It goes both ways.

How It Works

We oftem recive client request's for services out of our scope, or area of service. Instead of turning them away, we refer them to vetted contractors in our network. You get a warm written introduction to a qualified client. We take a referral fee based on the engagement type. After the no-cut-out period ends, the client is fully yours with no fee or obligation.

And when you run into work that's in our scope such as garden maintenance, planting, or yard cleanup - send them our way. We pay the same rate back to you.

This is a two-way referral network. You're not just paying us, as you can earn too. Same rate, same rules on both sides.

Three Engagement Types

The fee rate and no-cut-out period depend on how the job is structured. Both are confirmed in the written introduction.

Capital Project
10%
of total invoice, excluding taxes
  • When you pay: Within 14 days of receiving client payment
  • No-cut-out: 18 months from written introduction
  • After 18 months: Client is yours. No fee, no notice, no obligation.
  • Staged invoicing: Fee applies to each payment received
Seasonal Contract
8%
of total seasonal contract value
  • When you pay: Within 14 days of receiving client payment
  • No-cut-out: Through the end of the first contracted season
  • Season 2 onward: Client is fully yours. No fee, no notice, no restriction on renewals.
One-Off Job
6%
of total invoice, including 7-day touch-up work
  • When you pay: Within 14 days of receiving client payment
  • No-cut-out: 7 days after job completion
  • On Day 8: Fully clear. No fee, no notice, no restriction.

No-cut-out clock starts on written introduction - not the quote date, the first invoice, or the first payment. This is stated on our Referral Notification Form.

If a Referred Client Contacts You Directly

If a referred client reaches out to you directly during the no-cut-out period for new work, additional improvements, or anything else, notify us in writing within 5 business days. This applies whether or not you take the work.

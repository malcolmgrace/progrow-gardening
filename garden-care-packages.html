Garden Care Packages

Three packages, one straightforward goal: your garden stays tidy, healthy, and under control. Choose the level that fits your garden.

Package 1
Essential Garden Care
Small residential gardens, low complexity
Frequency
  • Bi-weekly or monthly (May to October)
  • 45 to 60 min per visit
What's Included
  • Bed weeding (surface and visible roots)
  • Edge touch-up (beds only)
  • Deadheading annuals and perennials
  • Light debris removal
  • Visual plant health monitoring (report only)
Not Included
  • Shrub pruning
  • Dividing or transplanting
  • Soil amendments or mulch
  • Pest or disease treatment

Package 2 - Most Popular
Professional Garden Maintenance
Most homeowners, townhomes, small commercial properties
Frequency
  • Bi-weekly (May to October)
  • 60 to 90 min per visit
What's Included
  • Everything in Essential, plus:
  • Shrub pruning (seasonal, size-controlled)
  • Perennial cutbacks (as seasonally appropriate)
  • Minor staking and plant support
  • Weed suppression touch-ups
  • Garden condition reporting (email)
Not Included
  • Major rejuvenation pruning
  • Plant replacement
  • New bed creation
  • Mulch or topsoil supply

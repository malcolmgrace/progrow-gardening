Scheduled Garden Maintenance for Ottawa Homeowners

ProGrow offers three maintenance packages for residential and small commercial properties in Nepean, Barrhaven, Riverside South, Manotick, and Kanata. Each package is flat-rate, based on the size and complexity of your garden beds, with visits scheduled on a recurring basis from April through November.

Pricing shown is a starting point. Your actual rate is confirmed after a site review. Gardens in rough condition require a one-time cleanup visit before recurring service can begin.

Have questions about what your garden needs? Visit our Ottawa Gardening FAQ for guidance on maintenance schedules, soil care, and seasonal timing.

Before You Book

Service Conditions

Please review the following before submitting a quote request. These conditions apply to all packages and are in place to make sure recurring service runs smoothly for both parties.

  • Flat-rate pricing. All packages are priced per visit based on your garden's size and complexity. Your rate is confirmed after a site assessment, not before. Starting prices listed are the minimum for qualifying properties.
  • Minimum service charge applies. A minimum visit charge applies to all bookings regardless of work volume. One-off visits are not offered under these packages.
  • Overgrown gardens require a cleanup first. If your garden beds have not been maintained recently, a one-time initial cleanup visit is required before recurring service begins. This is quoted and billed separately.
  • Work is completed within the scheduled visit window. Each visit has an allotted time. Tasks are prioritized within that window. Requests for additional work beyond the package scope are quoted separately.
  • Recurring commitment required. These packages are designed for ongoing seasonal service, not single visits. Consistent scheduling is what keeps gardens manageable and pricing stable.
  • Garden beds only. ProGrow focuses on ornamental garden beds, shrubs, and perennials. Lawn care, grass cutting, and turf maintenance are not offered.
  • Access to the property is required on visit days. Please ensure beds are accessible and pets are secured prior to each scheduled visit.
